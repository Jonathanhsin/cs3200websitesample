Welcome to Minority Meals

Minority Meals is a project created by Northeasten students, Priyanka Chowdhury, Caitlin Hall, Jonathan Hsin, Apurva Jain, Valerie Robert, to bring attention to minority owned restaurants which have been heavily impacted by the current pandemic.
